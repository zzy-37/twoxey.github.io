<span lang="cn">
一场突如其来的疫情加速了全领域的数字化转型，在过去的一年多里，数字化和智能技术的无缝渗透在不知不觉中改变了我们的工作和生活习惯，原子与比特世界的界面变得越来越模糊。  
从生产到生活，从医疗、出行到休闲娱乐、文化体验，“混合时空”板块中的25位毕业生试图探索数字化和智能技术在多样场景中的全新应用，从各自的视角诠释物理与虚拟世界加速融合的未来新常态。

<span lang="en">
The sudden occurrence of the pandemic has accelerated digital transformation of all fields. For the past year, the seamless infiltration of digital and intelligent technology has unknowingly changed our workflow and lifestyle, and the interface between the atom world and the bit world has begun to blur.  
From the industry to the everyday, from healthcare and travel to entertainment and cultural experience, 25 graduates in the "Hybrid Space-Time" session are trying to explore new applications of digital and intelligent technology in diverse scenarios, and interpret from their own perspectives the future new normal of accelerating intertwinement between physical and virtual worlds.

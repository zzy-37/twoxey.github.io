cn: 科技、产业和经济的高速发展在惠及个体和社会的同时，也伴随着一系列复杂问题的产生：时间的碎片化、人口的老龄化、媒体的娱乐化、竞争的激烈化……在一个不断迭新的技术经济时代，在满足发展需求的同时，设计可以如何为人的健康和福祉做出更多努力与贡献？“普惠关怀”板块中的35位毕业生在各自的设计中展现了对不同社会群体的关注与研究，试图以多样的设计触点促成对于人们⽣理与精神福祉的包容普惠。
en: "The rapid development of technology, industry and economy has benefited individuals as well as the whole society, but has also been accompanied by a series of complex problems: the fragmentation of time, the aging of population, the pan-entertainment of mass media, the intensification of competition, etc. While meeting development needs in an ever-updating era of technology-based economy, how can design make more efforts and contributions to human wellbeing and welfare? 35 graduates in the “Inclusive Wellness” session are showing their concerns and research on different social groups in their respective designs, trying to facilitate the inclusive solicitude for physiological and mental wellbeing through a variety of design touchpoints."

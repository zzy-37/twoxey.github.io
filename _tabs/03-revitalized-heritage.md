<span lang="cn">
经济的腾飞发展总是伴生着快速的文化迭代，许多优秀的文化遗产在飞进的时代浪潮中被渐渐淡忘乃至慢慢消失。  
传统文化如何在当代生活中重焕新的活力；新兴消费场景和创新商业模式，在这一过程中又可以扮演怎样的角色？  
在文字、艺术、工艺、民俗等丰富的中华文化宝藏中，“焕新传承”板块中的25位毕业生尝试以各自的方式建立起新旧对话的桥梁，探索用设计推动传统文化在当代社会经济背景下的新发展。

<span lang="en">
Rapid development of the economy is always accompanied by rapid cultural iterations, and many outstanding cultural heritages are gradually forgotten and even disappeared in the ever-changing era.  
How can traditional culture regain its vitality in contemporary life, and what role can emerge consumption scenarios and innovative business models play in this process?  
Among the rich Chinese cultural treasures, such as Chinese characters, art, crafts, and folklore, 25 graduates in the “Revitalized Heritage” session are trying to rebuild a bond between the old and the new in separate ways, and explore the application of design in boosting the new development of traditional culture in the contemporary social and economic background.

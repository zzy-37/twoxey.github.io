cn: 常态化的疫情防控要求拉开了物理的人际距离，而移动互联网与智能终端则架起了泛在的数字通道。在商业与文化生态系统被新形式的公共连结刷新后，设计师应该以怎样的立场和手段，参与乃至发动社群的资源共享、知识共创和平台共建？从品牌经济到公共文化，“协作社群”板块中的15位毕业生针对不同领域、不同形式的社群展开研究，探索设计如何促成商业与文化社群的繁荣与协同。
en: Normalized pandemic prevention requirements have extended physical interpersonal distance, while mobile Internet and smart terminals have set up ubiquitous digital channels. After the commercial and cultural ecosystems are refreshed by new forms of public connections, what position and means should designers take to participate and initiate resource sharing, knowledge co-creation, and platform co-construction in a community? From brand economy to public culture, 15 graduates in the “Synergetic Community” session have conducted research on communities of different fields and different forms, exploring how design can promote the prosperity and collaboration of business and cultural communities.
